Goodfellas

The greatest gangster picture portrayed in the history of cinema. From the acting to music to cinematography, this film is an overall perfect classic piece of great cinema. Scorsese at the top of his mastery in filmmaking.

The Seventh Seal

A profoundly moving tale of doubt, faith and humanity. It is richly complex and indluges us to confront the nature of humanity. Poignant, lyrical and moving. This is what I appraise as the best film Ingmar Bergman has ever made.

The 400 Blows

Poetically sincere and moving, one of those films that capture the essence of childhood. Truffaut is a raw voice of filmic talent.
